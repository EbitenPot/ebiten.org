We renamed Ebiten to Ebitengine

Hajime Hoshi
2022-05-26

In order to make our engine more unique, we have decided to rename Ebiten to Ebitengine! Ebitengine is a portmanteau of an Ebiten and an Engine, and is pronounced like Ebi-Ten-Gin.

We don't change the website URL and the GitHub repository names. You never have to change your source code.

Thank you for your continuous support!
